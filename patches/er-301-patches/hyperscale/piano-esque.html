Multi-Sample Piano with Grain Stretch & Feedback Looper

piano esque

Multi-velocity Kawai 9' Grand piano sampler with grain stretching for textural manipulation, feedback looper for ambient layers, and autogen mode for generative piano textures.

Sample Playback

  • Samples: 42 Kawai 9' Grand samples (Hard + Medium velocity)
  • Stretch: Time-stretch/pitch-shift amount
  • Duration: Note sustain length
  • Velocity: Hard/Medium sample blend based on velocity

Autogen Section

  • BPM: Clock tempo for auto-triggering
  • Clk Mult: Clock multiplier
  • Trig Prob: Probability of note triggering
  • Pan Rnd: Random stereo panning

Grain Stretch

  • Stretch: Pitch/time stretch ratio
  • Grain Dur: Individual grain length
  • Grain Mix: Dry/stretched blend

Feedback Looper

  • Looper: Feedback looper wet level
  • Loop Time: Delay time for looper
  • Feedback: Loop feedback amount

Tone Section

  • Tone: Low-pass filter brightness
  • Del: Delay wet level
  • Del Time: Delay time
  • Del Fdbk: Delay feedback

MIDI Control

  • Notes: Play piano notes (velocity-sensitive)
  • Mod Wheel: Grain stretch amount
  • CC 2: Tone/brightness

Keyboard

  • Space: Toggle autogen
  • Z-M: Play piano notes

Original ER-301 Unit

Based on piano esque.pkg from mudlogger's Hyperscale collection featuring Variable Speed Player, Grain Stretch, and Feedback Looper with Kawai 9' Grand samples.
