Dual Oscillator Synth with Ring Mod, Filter, Delay & Reverb

OTA Synthesizer

A comprehensive West Coast-inspired synthesizer featuring dual aliasing triangle oscillators, ring modulation, noise sources, and a rich effects chain.

Signal Flow

Two oscillators with optional sync pass through a ring modulator with adjustable frequency. White and pink noise can be mixed in. The signal is shaped by a Bique bandpass filter with ADSR envelope, then processed through spread delay and Dattorro reverb with CPR compression.

Oscillator Section

  • Pitch: Master pitch in V/Oct (MIDI controlled)
  • Freq 1/2: Fine frequency adjustment for each oscillator
  • Vol 1/2: Individual oscillator volumes
  • Oct 1/2: Octave shift per oscillator
  • Sync: Hard sync amount (Osc 2 syncs to Osc 1)
  • Slew: Portamento amount

Ring Modulator

  • RM: Ring modulation depth
  • RM Freq: Ring modulator carrier frequency (0-2000 Hz)

Noise Section

  • Noise: Overall noise level
  • White: White noise amount
  • Pink: Pink noise amount

Modulation Envelope (Arc AD)

  • Mod Env: Envelope to filter amount
  • Cycle: Auto-cycling (LFO mode)
  • Rise/Fall: Attack and decay times
  • Bend: Envelope curve shape

Filter (Bique)

  • F0: Filter cutoff frequency
  • Q: Resonance
  • F Gain: Filter output gain

Amplitude Envelope

  • A/D/S/R: Standard ADSR envelope

Effects

  • D Time: Delay time
  • D Spread: Stereo spread
  • D Fdbk: Delay feedback
  • D Wet: Delay mix
  • R Decay: Reverb decay time
  • R Damp: Reverb damping
  • R W/D: Reverb wet/dry mix

MIDI Control

  • Notes: Trigger and pitch control
  • Velocity: Affects filter envelope amount
  • Mod Wheel: Filter cutoff
  • Aftertouch: Ring mod depth

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Space: Trigger note
  • Z-M: Play notes (keyboard piano style)
  • 1-9: Quick presets

Original ER-301 Unit

Based on ota.pkg from mudlogger's Hyperscale collection. Features dual Aliasing Triangle oscillators, Scale Quantizer, Ring Modulator, Bique filter, Arc AD envelope, ADSR, Spread Delay, Dattorro Reverb, and CPR compression.
