OTA Rev 2

An enhanced generative synthesizer building on OTA with autogen mode, scale quantization, Low Pass Gate, and advanced noise shaping.

New in Rev 2

  • Autogen Mode: Self-generating patterns triggered by internal clock
  • Clock with Mul/Div: Tempo-synced triggering with multiplier/divider
  • Scale Quantizer: Constrain pitches to musical scales
  • Trigger Probability: Probabilistic note triggering
  • Strike LPG: West Coast Low Pass Gate with rise/fall/bend
  • Bandpass Noise: Filtered noise added post-oscillators
  • Tanh Saturation: Warm drive on the output

Autogen Section

  • Autogen: Enable self-generating mode
  • BPM: Master tempo (40-200 BPM)
  • Mul/Div: Clock multiplier and divider
  • Trig Prob: Probability of each trigger firing
  • Scale: Musical scale for pitch quantization
  • Offset: Root note offset in semitones

Oscillator Section

  • Freq 1/2: Fine frequency adjustment
  • Vol 1/2: Individual oscillator volumes
  • Oct 1/2: Octave shift per oscillator
  • Osc Sync: Hard sync amount

LPG (Low Pass Gate)

  • LPG Rise: Attack time
  • LPG Fall: Decay time
  • LPG Bend: Envelope curve
  • LPG Hght: Maximum gate opening

Noise Section

  • Noise: Pre-oscillator noise
  • P Noise: Post-oscillator noise
  • BP Freq/Q: Bandpass filter for post noise

MIDI Control

  • Notes: Pitch and trigger (overrides autogen)
  • Mod Wheel: Filter cutoff
  • CC 2: LPG Fall time

Keyboard

  • Space: Toggle autogen
  • Z-M: Play notes manually
  • 1-5: Quick scale presets

Original ER-301 Unit

Based on ota rev 2.pkg from mudlogger's Hyperscale collection. Adds Clocked Random Gate, Scale Quantizer, Strike LPG, bandpass noise, and autogen mode to the original OTA.
