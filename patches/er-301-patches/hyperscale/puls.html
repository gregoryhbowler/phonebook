Generative Pulse Synth with Random Gate, HP Filter & Dattorro Reverb

Puls Synthesizer

A generative pulse synthesizer featuring a sine oscillator with randomized pitch modulation, probability-based triggering, and lush effects. Creates rhythmic, organic pulse patterns.

Signal Flow

A sine oscillator is gated by a Clocked Random Gate with probability control. Random pitch modulation creates melodic variation, smoothed by a slew limiter. The signal passes through a high-pass Bique filter (also with random frequency modulation), then through spread delay and Dattorro reverb with CPR compression.

Oscillator Section

  • Pitch (posc): Base oscillator pitch in V/Oct
  • Octave: Octave shift (-4 to +4)
  • RPitch: Random pitch modulation amount (0-0.45)

Gate & Envelope

  • Puls (Prob): Gate probability (0-1). Higher = more triggers
  • A (Attack): Very fast attack time (0-30ms)
  • D (Decay): Fast decay time (0-40ms)

High-Pass Filter (Bique)

  • HP Freq: Base filter frequency (pitch-tracked)
  • HP Q: Filter resonance (0-0.5)

Spread Delay

  • D Time: Delay time (0-1s)
  • D Spread: Stereo spread (-1 to +1)
  • D Wet: Delay mix

Dattorro Reverb

  • R BW (Bandwidth): Pre-reverb filtering
  • R Dec (Decay): Reverb tail length
  • R Damp: High frequency damping
  • R Wet: Reverb mix

Output

  • Vol: Master volume

Auto-Trigger Mode

Enable AUTO to let the internal clock generate pulses automatically. The Clocked Random Gate uses probability to decide which clock pulses become note triggers, creating evolving rhythmic patterns.

MIDI Control

  • Notes: Trigger and pitch control (C2 and above)
  • Velocity: Affects attack envelope
  • Mod Wheel (CC1): HP filter frequency
  • CC2: Probability

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Space: Trigger note
  • Z-M: Play chromatic notes

Original ER-301 Unit

Based on puls.pkg from mudlogger's Hyperscale collection. Features Sine Oscillator, Clocked Random Gate, Pingable Scaled Random, Slew Limiter, ADSR, Bique HP filter, Spread Delay, Dattorro Reverb, and CPR compression.
