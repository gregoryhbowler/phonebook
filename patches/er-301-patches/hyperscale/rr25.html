R+R25 Synthesizer

A self-generating chaotic synthesizer inspired by Rob Hordijk's Blippoo Box and the Rungler circuit. Named after Richard (D. James) and Rob (Hordijk), this patch creates evolving, unpredictable sonic textures through cross-modulated oscillators and a shift register "rungler".

How It Works

Two oscillators cross-modulate each other's frequencies, creating chaotic FM interactions. A 16-step shift register (Rungler) samples one oscillator and uses it to modulate pitch and filter parameters. The result is self-evolving generative music that never repeats.

Oscillator Section

  • Richard (Osc 1): Square wave oscillator frequency (0-500 Hz)
  • Rob (Osc 2): Saw wave oscillator frequency (10-500 Hz)
  • XMod 1: Cross-modulation from Osc 2 to Osc 1
  • XMod 2: Cross-modulation from Osc 1 to Osc 2

Rungler Section

  • Length: Shift register length (1-64 steps)
  • Stride: Step size through register
  • Drift: Random variation amount
  • R Mod: Rungle to oscillator modulation depth

Filter (Bique)

  • Q: Filter Q/resonance modulation
  • Res: Base resonance amount

Effects

  • Delay: Feedback delay network
  • Spread: Stereo spread delay
  • Reverb: Freeverb with chaos modulation

Tips

  • Start with Richard and Rob near similar frequencies for beating effects
  • Increase XMod values for more chaotic behavior
  • Lower Stride values create more repetitive patterns
  • This is a self-generating patch - just press play and explore
